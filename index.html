Table top Simulator

Development of a prototype to play table-top games. We used the user-centered design from the start to arrive at our final solution. The objective of this project was to create a new application for players to play their favourite table top games. The motivation to do this project came from the COVID19, which prevented players from meeting and playing. Firstly, we went to a ‚Äúgame caf√©‚Äù in Madrid to observe the players and understand the different types of interaction to ensure we understood our audience. In that same caf√© we asked the players some questions, that would help us shape a first version of our system. With this information, we discussed as a group the devices where we would implement this solution. Originally, the idea was to use a table-like screen as a virtual table with the game and another device (such as a mobile phone) to virtually simulate the cards. Due to COVID, we had to prototype something easier and we opted for a smartphone and tablet design. We developed two low-fidelity prototypes and ran some usability tests virtually, for both devices. After this step it was clear the tablet design had less problems. We proceeded with it and made a high-fidelity prototype in Android. Finally, we tested the high-fidelity design with a selection of user tasks.
